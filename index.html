The Sport

What Is
Cyclocross?

Cyclocross blends the speed of road racing, the grit of mountain biking, and a dash of pure chaos. Short, punchy laps. Mud. Barriers. Cheering crowds. It's the most fun you can have on a bike in the fall.

⚡

Fast & Furious

Races last 40–60 minutes — short enough to go all-out the entire time. Every lap is a full-throttle effort from start to finish.

🌿

Mixed Terrain

Courses mix pavement, grass, dirt, and trail — with steep run-ups, tight corners, and obstacles that send you off the bike mid-race.

🏆

All Levels Welcome

From first-timers to elite racers, UTCX has categories for every age and ability. Show up, pin a number, and see what you've got.

📍

Wasatch Front Venues

Races roll through some of Utah's best parks and venues from Salt Lake to Weber County — new courses every week.

🚵

Any Bike Works

Cyclocross bike, mountain bike, road bike — whatever you have works. Just make sure it has working brakes and no bar-ends.

👥

Community First

UTCX is as much a community as it is a race series. Expect cowbells, heckling, post-race beer hand-ups, and lifelong friends.
