Who Can Register in the Men's/Open Category

  • Any individual, regardless of sex assigned at birth, is allowed to register in any of the Men's/Open category. USA Cycling
  • There are still rulebook/regulation constraints, etc., but from the gender/sex perspective, there is no restriction: male, female, or other — everyone can enter Men's/Open. USA Cycling

Who Can Register in the Women's Category

  • The Women's category is limited to individuals who are female. And "female" is defined as: "a person belonging, at conception, to the sex that produces the large reproductive cell." USA Cycling
