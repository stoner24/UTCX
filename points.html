Series Points
& Scoring

Your top 8 results count toward the overall season standings.

Race your 10 races, drop your two worst results, and your top 8 count. All categories' finishers outside the top 20 receive 1 point just for finishing. Chase the series, or just show up and race — either way you're earning points.

Top 8 Count. Race all 10 — your two lowest scores are dropped automatically. Only your best 8 results count toward the series standings.

State Championship Bonus

The final race of the season is the Utah State Championship. Points count toward the series standings as normal — there is no multiplier, but the championship title is awarded separately to the top finishers in each category.

Tiebreaker

In the event of a tie in series points, the tiebreaker is determined by the higher placing at the most recent race. If still tied, the next most recent race is used, and so on.
